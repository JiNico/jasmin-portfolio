## About

A 2D puzzle platformer that equates calories consumed to POWER. Eat food and push obstacles out of your way or to your benefit to complete each level.

Developed independently in a span of ~2 days with Kenney assets.


Created for the Kenney Jam 2025.

Made in Unity 3D.
