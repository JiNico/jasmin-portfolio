## About

In this 2D platformer, there are no power-ups and no stomping on enemies. Rely solely on your skills and resources to defy the system and get to the finish with as many coins as you can and as healthy as you can be. All while avoiding the accumulation of debt...

Can you find the balance between health and finances?

Made in Unity 3D.
