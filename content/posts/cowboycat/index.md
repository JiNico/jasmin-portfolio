## About

As a decorated sheriff of this here town, orderliness is a must! While on patrol, you find yourself in front of the town's saloon, and the thought of a nice cold beverage attracts you. The sun had been beating down on you all day, and you surely believe that you should... nay... DESERVE a cold beverage. Who would've thought getting yourself a glass of cold milk would result in a duel?

Hope you're quick on the draw.

In this text-based game, use your quick reflexes to defeat your enemy.

Made in Unity 3D.
